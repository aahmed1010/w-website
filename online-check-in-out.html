Key Capabilities & Limitations

Discover how our online check-in/check-out solutions can transform your business

cloud

Oracle Capabilities

  • Oracle Opera APIs provide basic check-in and check-out functionality.
  • Features include passport scanning and digital key issuance.
error_outline

Limitations

  • Oracle lacks multi-channel support, such as WhatsApp or Telegram integration.
  • Automated identity verification is minimal and often requires manual intervention.
psychology

LLM Enhancement

  • Natural Language Interaction: Guests can check in via conversational AI on platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, or web chat.
    Example: Guest: "I want to check in." Bot: "Please upload your passport." The bot processes the ID, confirms the check-in, and provides the digital key.
  • Proactive Guidance: LLMs can provide instant responses to FAQs like "What time is my room ready?" or "Can I get an early check-in?"
auto_awesome

Betterment

  • AI seamlessly handles multi-channel guest interactions.
  • Fully automated verification reduces wait times and errors, enhancing the guest experience.
  • Eliminates the need for reception visits, creating a completely contactless journey.
